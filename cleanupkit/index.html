Some information is shared with us directly by you — for instance, when you reach out to our support team. A limited amount of data is also gathered automatically to keep the App running smoothly and to help us identify and fix issues.

We may collect basic technical details from your device, including your IP address, language preferences, time zone, device type and model, operating system, and internet service provider. This helps us maintain compatibility and troubleshoot any issues that arise.

2.2.2. App Usage Information

We may log general usage signals — such as crash reports or feature interaction patterns — to help us understand how the App performs and where it can be improved.

2.2.3. Vault Authentication

If you set up a PIN, Face ID, or Touch ID to secure your vault, those credentials are managed entirely by your device's operating system. They are never transmitted to us or stored on any external server.

2.2.4. AI Features Disclosure

Where the App includes AI-powered features such as photo sorting or enhancement, all of that processing takes place directly on your device.
