Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Introduction

Live Translation: AI Translate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application. By using Live Translation: AI Translate, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Audio Data

  • Audio Input: We do not store any audio data. Audio is processed in real-time for transcription and translation purposes only.

Transcription and Translation Data

  • Text Data: Transcriptions and translations are processed within the Application and are not retained after your session ends. No transcriptions or translated text are stored in external databases.

Speech-to-Text Services

We utilize trusted third-party providers for real-time speech-to-text transcription. These services require an internet connection, as audio is processed on secure servers to generate accurate transcriptions. Our providers adhere to strict privacy standards:

  • No Model Training: Your audio and transcripts are never used to train or improve speech recognition models.
  • No Data Retention: Audio and transcript data are not stored by our providers — processing occurs in real-time only.
  • Encryption: All communication is encrypted using TLS 1.2+ protocols.

Translation Services

We utilize industry-leading third-party services for translation. These providers maintain high standards for data security and privacy protection, ensuring your data is processed in accordance with strict enterprise privacy practices. They receive only the minimum data necessary to perform the translation and are contractually obligated not to retain or misuse your data.

Analytics and Usage Data

  • Device and Usage Data: We may collect limited, non-personal data such as device model, locale/language, iOS version, app version, feature usage, and performance metrics.
  • Purpose: This data helps us understand how the app is used and improve reliability and user experience.
  • No Personal Data: Analytics data is not linked to personal information.

Microphone Access

The Application requires access to your device's microphone solely to enable live translation and transcription features. Microphone access is only active while you are actively using these features and is never used for background recording.

Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security measures to protect your data:

  • No Audio Storage: We do not store, record, or retain any of your audio data on our servers. Audio is streamed strictly for the purpose of real-time processing.
  • Secure Transmission: All communication with our third-party service providers is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+ protocols.
  • No Server Storage: We do not maintain any servers that store your audio, transcription, or translation data.
  • Platform Security: The Application uses standard platform-level security protections provided by iOS to safeguard your information.

While we implement these security measures, no system is completely impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

Since the Application does not collect or store personal data on external servers, we do not retain user data. Session audio and transcription data are discarded once your session ends.

If you uninstall the Application, any locally stored data is removed from your device according to your device's operating system policies.
